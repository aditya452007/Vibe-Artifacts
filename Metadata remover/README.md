## 🌟 Overview

**Metadata Remover** is a secure, client-side web application designed to protect your privacy by scrubbing invisible metadata (EXIF data) from your photos. Whether it's GPS coordinates, camera settings, or timestamps, this tool ensures that when you share a photo, you're only sharing the image itself.

## 🚀 Features

*   **🔒 Privacy First**: All processing happens in your browser. Your images are **never** uploaded to a server.
*   **📍 GPS Stripping**: Automatically detects and removes location data to prevent doxxing.
*   **📷 Deep Clean**: Removes device models, shutter speeds, timestamps, and software info.
*   **✨ Aesthetic UI**: A calming, premium interface with support for Light and Dark modes.
*   **📂 Batch Processing**: Drag and drop multiple images to clean them all at once.

## 🎨 Visual Identity

The project follows a "Calm Trust" aesthetic:

*   **Primary Colors**: Slate Blue (`#4A5568`) & Soft Teal (`#2C7A7B`).
*   **Typography**: Inter (Clean, geometric, legible).
*   **Experience**: Smooth fade transitions, no aggressive warnings, just confidence.

## 🛠️ Usage

1.  **Open the App**: Launch `index.html` in your browser.
2.  **Upload**: Drag and drop your images into the designated zone.
3.  **Review**: See exactly what hidden data is being removed (Location, Device, etc.).
4.  **Clean & Download**: Click "Remove Metadata" and download your safe, clean copies.
